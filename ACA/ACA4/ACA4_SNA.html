Definition and Interpretation of the network:¶

Definition: This is a network of friends that is connected through individual airlines choices/past flight experience. The nodes are 21 participants' name took part in the survey. The edges are drawn with the number of common airlines between each two edge.

Interpretation: We can assume participants' potential connection with one another, and their orientation towards certain airlines. The number of edges each node has represents the number of participants who have chosen the same airline. Thus, the more edges one node has, the greater diversity of airlines one took in relation to others, and the greater probability one will choose either airline in the future(less predictive of choosing a certain airline). On the other hand, the less edge one has, the more predictive of its airline preference.
